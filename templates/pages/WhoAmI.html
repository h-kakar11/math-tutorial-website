About me & K4Maths.com

My name is Hamim, the creator of K4Maths.com. I'm simply just a student - like you - passionate about making Maths and Physics accessible to everyone at any skill level. I started this website to share my knowledge, and help others excel in these subjects, especially considering how difficult it can be sometimes.

All the content on the website took me a very long time to create; countless hours of refinement and dedication went into making these tutorials as clear and helpful as possible.

If you have any questions, suggestions, or just want to say hi, feel free to get in touch. I'm always happy to hear from fellow learners and/or educators.
