What is a series?

A series (or summation) is just the sum of a sequence of numbers. For example, the series 1 + 2 + 3 + 4 + 5 is the sum of the first five natural numbers.
The way we would write this series is like this: $$\sum_{k=1}^{5} n = 1 + 2 + 3 + 4 + 5$$ This just means: "Add all numbers from the bottom number (1) to the top number (5)".
